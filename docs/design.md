# Aqua Nova - Game Design Document

## Overview
Aqua Nova is an underwater exploration and adventure game where the player navigates through aquatic environments, discovers creatures, and uncovers secrets.

## Core Gameplay Loop
1. Explore underwater environments
2. Collect items and resources
3. Encounter and interact with creatures
4. Solve simple puzzles
5. Progress through levels

## Player Character
- **Movement**: 8-directional movement
- **Health System**: Health bar, can take damage from enemies/hazards
- **Abilities**: Basic movement, interaction with objects
- **Upgrades**: Health increases, speed boosts

## Enemies
- **Basic Fish**: Patrol enemies, simple movement
- **Jellyfish**: Stationary hazards that damage on contact
- **Boss Creatures**: Stronger enemies with patterns

## World Design
- **Environments**: 
  - Coral Reef
  - Deep Trench
  - Kelp Forest
  - Underwater Caves
  
- **Features**:
  - Collectibles (pearls, crystals)
  - Breakable objects
  - Platforms and obstacles

## Progression
- Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
- Level-based progression
- Simple story elements

## Art Style
- Pixel art utilizing Pico-8's 16-color palette
- Underwater theme with blues, greens, and purples
- 8x8 sprite characters and enemies

## Controls
- **Arrow Keys / WASD**: Movement
- **Z / C**: Action (interact, attack)
- **X**: Cancel/Menu

## Future Features
- Multiple characters
- Weapons/combat system
- NPCs with dialogue
- Inventory system
- Sound and music
